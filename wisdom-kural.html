Kural · 1  |  Chapter 1: In Praise of God

அகர முதல எழுத்தெல்லாம் ஆதி
பகவன் முதற்றே உலகு.

Agara mudhala ezhuttellam aadhi
Bagavan mudharre ulagu.

As the letter 'A' is the first of all letters, so the eternal God is first in the world. Just as every letter of the alphabet begins with the sound "A," the universe itself begins with and rests upon the primordial, eternal Lord.
